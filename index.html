What It Is

A session generator
built for activity teams.

Resident Detective is a web tool for senior living staff. Enter a theme, the names of the residents who will play, and how long the session runs. The AI writes a complete, original mystery around that exact group — and formats everything as printable documents.

The entire activity runs on paper and conversation. Residents never touch a screen, download an app, or create an account.

🧠
Cognitive Engagement
Deduction, memory recall, and storytelling — structured thinking wrapped in a low-pressure game.
👥
Social Connection
Each resident plays a named character. Solving the mystery requires trading clues and talking with each other.
⚡
Minutes of Prep
Writing a custom group activity by hand takes hours. The tool generates one in seconds and prints in minutes.

Character sheets, facilitator guide, and clue cards print on standard paper. No tablets, logins, or devices for residents.

How It Works

Four steps, start to finish.

Steps advance automatically — or click any step to jump there.

💻
Step 01 of 04
Staff Opens the App

Any team member opens Resident Detective in a web browser — no installation, no game design background, no training course. The interface walks through setup in a short guided flow designed around a busy activity calendar.

Access
Web browser, no install or download required
Who Uses It
Lifestyle Directors, Activity Coordinators, or any community staff member
Learning Curve
Guided UI — most staff are ready to generate on the first visit
Platform
Designed to run within LCS's existing Microsoft environment
⚙️
Step 02 of 04
Configure the Session

Staff choose a mystery theme, type in resident names, and pick a session length. Settings for cognitive level, mobility, and roleplay intensity shape what the AI writes — the same theme produces a very different session for a memory care group than for a puzzle club.

Theme Options
Built-in genres, or describe a custom theme in plain text
Resident Names
Each resident gets a character written specifically for them
Adaptability
Cognitive level, mobility, group size, and time all shape the output
Time to Configure
Typically under 3 minutes to fill out every field
✨
Step 03 of 04
AI Generates the Packet

One click, and the AI writes a complete, never-before-seen mystery: an original title and storyline, a character sheet for every named resident, a scene-by-scene facilitator guide, and optional clue cards — all consistent with each other and tailored to the group's settings.

Character Sheets
One per resident — name, backstory, clues they hold, secrets they keep
Facilitator Guide
Scene-by-scene script with discussion prompts and the full solution
Generation Time
Seconds — the full packet is ready to review immediately
Always Original
The same inputs never produce the same story twice
🎭
Step 04 of 04
Print & Run the Activity

Staff print the packet and hand out character sheets. The facilitator guide carries the whole session — what to say, when to reveal each clue, and how to land the final reveal. Residents solve the mystery together through conversation, deduction, and the clues in their hands.

Resident Tech Needed
None. Paper only — no iPads, logins, or screens
Session Length
30 to 90 minutes, matching what was configured
Facilitation
The guide is self-contained — staff can lead without rehearsal
Total Prep Time
Under 8 minutes from opening the app to packet in hand
